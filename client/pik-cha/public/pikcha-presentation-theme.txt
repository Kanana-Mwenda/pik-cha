Pik-Cha Presentation Theme Guide

- Background: Linear gradient from #6366f1 (indigo) to #a21caf (purple) to #06b6d4 (cyan)
- Headings: Orbitron font, bold, white or dark indigo
- Body: Inter font, white or dark gray
- Logo: Pik-Cha SVG logo (triangle with gradient) in top left of every slide
- Accent: Use rounded rectangles, soft drop shadows, and modern icons
- Buttons: Gradient fill, white text, rounded corners
- Slide layout: Clean, lots of whitespace, large headings
- Use icons for features, tech stack, and demo steps
- Consistent color palette: #6366f1, #a21caf, #06b6d4, #fff, #222
